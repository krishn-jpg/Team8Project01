History and Significance

The name Eloheh, which is a word that echoes the wisdom of the Cherokee language, signifies peace, wholeness, abundance, and harmony. These values are in the blood of our company since it was founded and are based on the ideas of Native Americans that evaluate the relationship between people, nature, and health. However, through the course of decades, Eloheh has changed, yet its mission has been unchanged, as it is based on ancient knowledge as well as on recent progress.

As our family name goes, the story about Eloheh started with the strong connection that the founders had with the land and the plants that surrounded them--plants that can be used to heal impurities and infirmities. Native American medicine was the guidance of the founders of Eloheh very long before pharmacies and medications as we might refer to them today. They knew the strength of nature, the herbs, the roots, and what was considered natural medication, and created herbal tonics and healing balms using the plants found in their land. Their founders belonged to a society that accorded special praise to the land owing to its riches, and their reality was that real health occurred when one was at peace with nature.
